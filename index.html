MovieSense: Empowering Movie Reviews with AI

Oct 2023 (Individual project)

MovieSense is a sophisticated NLP-driven solution designed to decode the sentiments of movie reviews. It provides concise summaries and bridges language gaps by translating reviews from English to French. Leveraging state-of-the-art algorithms and models, MovieSense aims to enhance users' comprehension of movie critiques.

Key Features:
  • Sentiment Analysis: Harnesses the power of the Naive Bayes Classifier to determine if movie reviews are positive or negative, using datasets from Rotten Tomatoes.
  • Dynamic Summarisation: Incorporates Facebook's BART model to distill lengthy reviews into short, comprehensible summaries.
  • Translation: Enables a broader audience reach by seamlessly translating reviews from English to French using the Helsinki-NLP model.
